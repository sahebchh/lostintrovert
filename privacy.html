Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Cookies

Analytics

This site does not currently use any analytics or tracking software. No data about your visit is collected or stored by us.

Advertising

This site does not currently display advertisements. If advertising is introduced in the future, this policy will be updated.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated.
