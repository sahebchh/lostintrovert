About

For those who feel too much about films

I'm Sahebjit Chhabra — based in Delhi, and I've been making fan edits and video essays since 2019 under the name lostintrovert.

It started in 2019 with the How I Met Your Mother finale deleted scenes — a video that now has over 5 million views. The ZNMD poems video has 1.3 million. People find these videos through search, years after they were made, and send them to someone they love at 2am.

One of those videos — a character study of Laxman Pandey from Rang De Basanti — caught the attention of Atul Kulkarni, the actor who played the role. In November 2025 he posted it on his YouTube community tab with the caption "One of my favourites!" and replied to my comment: "Thank you so much for making it."

That's the whole thing. Content that finds the right person at the right moment. Deleted scenes that should have aired. Poems that name what you were feeling but couldn't say. Edits that make you feel the film all over again.
